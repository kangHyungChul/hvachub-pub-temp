Freestanding Solution​

Freestanding Retail Buildings are standalone structures designed exclusively for retail purposes, including formats like Big Box stores, Power Centers & Outlets, and Pop-Up Retail spaces. These buildings ​prioritize visibility, accessibility, and customer flow, often featuring large floor areas and flexible layouts.

When designing HVAC systems for freestanding retail buildings, key factors to consider include:

  • Efficient Cooling and Heating for Large Spaces
  • Adaptability to High Occupancy
  • Flexibility for Future Changes
  • Specialized Requirements

Non-freestanding Solution

Non-Freestanding Retail Buildings are multi-tenant retail spaces ​integrated within larger complexes, including Regional Shopping ​Centers, Community Shopping Centers, Neighborhood Shopping ​Centers, and Strip or Convenience Shopping Centers. These buildings​ serve as hubs for retail, dining, and entertainment, featuring shared ​common areas and parking facilities.​

When designing HVAC systems for non-freestanding retail buildings,​ key factors to consider include:

  • Zoned Systems for Multi-Tenant Layouts
  • Flexibility and Scalability
  • Noise Control
  • BMS Compatibility
