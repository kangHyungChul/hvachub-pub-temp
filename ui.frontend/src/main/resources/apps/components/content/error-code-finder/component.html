E010

DVM.DPM.DBS: Communication error between indoor and outdoor units (in case of failure to receive signals from the indoor unit). SINGLE: Communication error between indoor unit panel and main unit.

Posiible Causes

1. Signal Cable Issue: The signal cable connecting the indoor and outdoor units might be damaged or loosely connected.
2. Power Issue: If the power supply to the indoor or outdoor unit is unstable or disconnected, communication errors can occur.
3. Communication Module Issue: There may be a fault in the communication module of either the indoor or outdoor unit.
4. Electromagnetic Interference: Electromagnetic interference from the surrounding environment can disrupt signal transmission.

Solutions

1. Inspect and Replace Cables: Check the signal cable connecting the indoor and outdoor units and fix or replace any damaged or loose connections.
2. Verify Power Supply: Ensure that the power supply to both the indoor and outdoor units is stable and continuous.
3. Replace Communication Module: If there is a fault in the communication module, replace the faulty part.
4.Eliminate Electromagnetic Interference: Reduce electromagnetic interference by adjusting the placement of electronic devices near the air conditioner or installing protective measures.
