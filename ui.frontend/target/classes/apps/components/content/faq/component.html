  • Title 1 Upon withdrawal from membership

    Upon withdrawal from membership, termination will apply to all services subscribed under Samsung Account. Consequently, service usage history, download history, purchase records, membership tier and points, as well as coupons, will be permanently deleted.
    However, contents authored by members such as product reviews and comments will not be deleted from Samsung.com.
